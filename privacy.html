Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Rentleeys ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our service at rentleeys.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, password)
  • List a property (address, description, photos, pricing)
  • Make or receive a booking (dates, guest count, messages)
  • Make a payment (processed securely by PayPal)
  • Contact us for support

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect guests with hosts and process bookings
  • To send booking confirmations and notifications
  • To process payments securely
  • To verify host and guest identities
  • To improve our platform and services

3. Information Sharing

We share limited information between guests and hosts to facilitate bookings. We do not sell your personal data. We share data with:

  • Payment processors (PayPal) for secure transactions
  • Infrastructure providers (Supabase, Cloudflare, Railway)
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) in anonymized form

4. Google OAuth

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. We do not access your Google contacts, Drive, or other Google services.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Download your booking history
